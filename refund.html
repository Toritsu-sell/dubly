Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Our Commitment

We want you to be completely satisfied with our dubbing services. If you are not happy with the quality of our work we will do everything we can to make it right.

Refunds Before Work Begins

If you request a cancellation before we have started work on your videos you are entitled to a full refund. Please contact us within 24 hours of purchase at wertyjon2@gmail.com

Refunds After Delivery

If the delivered dubbing does not meet the agreed specifications or has significant quality issues we will offer either a free redo of the affected videos or a partial refund proportional to the affected work.

No Refund Situations

We do not offer refunds in the following situations: work has been completed and delivered as agreed, revisions have already been provided, or the issue is due to the quality of the original source video submitted.

How to Request a Refund

Contact us at wertyjon2@gmail.com with your order details and reason for the refund request. We respond to all refund requests within 2 business days.

Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days and returned to your original payment method via Paddle.

Contact

For refund requests or questions contact us at wertyjon2@gmail.com
